WHAT IS ACUPUNCTURE?

Acupuncture is an Ancient Chinese Medicine that uses knowledge of body energy (Qi) flow through meridians to identify blockages. I treat those blockages to restore the balance of the flow and return health to your body.

HOW DOES ACUPUNCTURE WORK?

Needles help to move the energy through the meridian and reconnect the energy (Qi) flow through the blockages. As well, at the site of the needle, the body produces its own natural "pain killer" (a substance known as endorphins) which gives Acupuncture additional additional power for treatment of disorders with pain as a major symptom.

CAN IT BE USED WITH OTHER THERAPIES?

Acupuncture can be combined with the following therapies: massage, physiotherapy, chiropractic homeopathic, etc. It can also be combined safely with most Western Medicine therapies. As a matter of fact, it is now used in some hospitals and dental linics for some procedures instead of traditional methods of sedating patients.
