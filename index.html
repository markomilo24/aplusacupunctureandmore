A+ Acupuncture and More

Dubravka (Deb) Milovanovic was born in Sarajevo, former Yugoslavia where she graduated with a degree in Western Medicine and earned her degree as a Doctor of Medicine in 1985. She worked as a Professor of Medical Subjects in three different schools (dental assistants, nurses and midwifes) for three years. In addition to this, she also volunteered at the Institute for Social Medicine and nutritional hygiene with the late Dr. Svetlana Zec, the only doctor nutritionist in Bosnia at the time.

In 1989, she recieved a scholarship to study Acupuncture and Traditional Chinese Medicine (TCM) in Tian Jin, P.R. China. Upon returning to Yugoslavia, she worked with four professors from China as a Medical Doctor and and Acupuncturist. She wrote a book on the integration of the two medicines but it was never published due to the Yugoslav Wars that broke out in 1992. Deb worked in Belgrade for one year for the European Center for Peace and Development and taught Acupuncture and TCM theory to Medical Doctors.

In 1993, she immigrated to Waterloo, Ontario where she still practices Acupuncture. Deb studied Homeopathic Medicine with Professor Ron Harris, the biggest name in Homeopathic Medicine in Canada. In 2010, she was trained to work with MSAS machines to test the environmental and food sensitivity causes.

In addition to Traditional Chinese Medicine and Homeopathy, Deb is a trained Auricular Medicine and Energetic Medicine practitioner since 2003. She studied in Canada under the very best in her fields of natural medicine; Dr. Michael Adams, N.D, Dr. Gueriot, Dr. Toudelier, Dr. Robert Abell, N.D, LAc, Thom Dickson, DDSND, and Dr. Nigel Plummer. While studying under these doctors, she learned how to use natural products in biotherapeutic drainage and address different medical conditions.

Deb has been registered with the College of Traditional Chinese Medicine and Acupuncture Practitioners of Ontario (CTCMPAO) since its beginning in April of 2013. She is in the process of getting her license from the College of Homeopaths of Ontario.
